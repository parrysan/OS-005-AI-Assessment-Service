02 — Economics & Upside

A ladder, not a brochure. Each rung opens the next.

The assessment is the door. The implementation work is the room behind it. This page covers both — tiered pricing for the front door, and the three paths that turn a €1,000 engagement into a multi-year relationship.

03 / The economics

From free pilots to €1,000 assessments, each rung filters a sharper client.

Pilot
Free
First three to five assessments, in exchange for a written testimonial and a public case study.
Growth
€200 — €500
Once social proof exists. Filters for owners who can act on what the report says.
Scale
€1,000+
Standard ticket. Premium framing. Owners who already know what they need next.
Implementation
€3k — €30k
Where the assessment turns into actual revenue. Three primary engagement types — below.
04 / Upside

Three implementation paths that turn one assessment into a multi-year relationship.

€3,000 — €5,000

Process optimization

Find the broken 15-step workflow before automating it. Restructure first, then apply the tools. The cleanest engagement: short, defensible, visibly time-saving.

€2,500 — €8,000

Speed-to-lead agents

Inbound enquiries answered in seconds rather than days. Direct, attributable revenue lift. Often the first place an owner sees AI pay for itself in the same quarter.

€1,500 — €3,000

Custom knowledge systems

A GPT trained on the company's own documents, products, and policies. Resolves the 95% of repeat customer questions that don't need a human in the loop.
